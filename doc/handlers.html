handlers

handlers

Source:
  • jquery.aria.js, line 671

Handlers for properties, references and states. Each handler has at least a get and set method to write and read the values. has methods check whether the property exists, unset removes the property.

handlers.reference and handlers.state defer to handlers.property (they don't inherit from handlers.property but they may do in another implementation - any functionality they don't have will be taken from handlers.property).
